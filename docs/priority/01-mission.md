# Mirror Factory — Mission & Values

## Mission
Mirror Factory builds AI-powered tools that make small teams operate like large ones. We believe the future of work is human-AI collaboration — where AI handles the operational overhead so humans can focus on strategy, creativity, and relationships.

## Core Values

### Quality Over Speed
We don't ship without tests. We don't cut corners to save time. A product that works reliably is worth more than a product that ships fast.

### Transparency
We tell clients the truth, even when it's uncomfortable. We share our reasoning, our doubts, and our process. No black boxes.

### Ownership
Every team member owns their work end-to-end. No "that's not my job." If you see a problem, you fix it or flag it.

### Continuous Learning
We stay current with technology. We experiment. We share what we learn. The team that stops learning stops being relevant.

## What We Won't Compromise On
- Client data privacy and security
- Honest communication with clients and each other
- Testing before shipping
- Documenting decisions and their reasoning
