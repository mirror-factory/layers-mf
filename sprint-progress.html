Product & Sprint Review — March 9, 2026

What Layers Does

The user journey from signup to daily use

1
Sign up & create your org
Email + password signup at /signup. Creates your organization and first member automatically. You land on an empty Context Library.
↓
2
Bring your knowledge in
Upload PDFs, DOCX, TXT, Markdown directly. Or Connect Google Drive, GitHub, Slack, Linear, Granola via OAuth. Each document is AI-extracted (title, summaries, entities) and vector-embedded for search.
↓
3
Chat with your knowledge
Agentic AI chat at /chat. Pick from 7 models (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini). The agent searches your context library, retrieves full documents, and synthesizes answers with citations. Multi-step tool calling with up to 6 reasoning steps.
↓
4
Organize into Sessions
Create named workspaces at /sessions. Link specific documents to a session — chat is then scoped only to those docs. Great for projects, research threads, or deep-dives.
↓
5
Stay on top of action items
Inbox at /inbox — AI automatically surfaces action items, decisions, and mentions from every document you upload or sync. Daily cron generates prioritized inbox items (urgent/high/normal/low).
↓
6
Monitor quality
Analytics at /analytics — three tabs: Context Health (ingestion success, embedding coverage), Agent Analytics (search quality, error rates, token usage), Retrieval Quality (search scoring).
